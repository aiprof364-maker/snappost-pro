### **2. Core Functionality - What SnapPost Pro Does**

SnapPost Pro is a specialized tool for contractors and tradespeople with these specific features:

**Primary Function:**
Contractors upload job site photos → AI generates professional captions → Posts branded content to Facebook Business Page

**Step-by-Step Workflow:**
1. **Photo Upload** — Contractor captures or uploads a job site photo (roofing, deck, landscaping, plumbing work, etc.)
2. **AI Caption Generation** — System automatically creates a professional, branded caption tailored to the contractor's business
3. **Logo Overlay** — Company logo is applied to the photo for consistent branding
4. **Facebook Publishing** — Content is posted directly to the contractor's Facebook Business Page with a single click

**Target Users:**
- Contractors (roofing, deck building, landscaping, plumbing, electrical)
- Construction professionals
- Tradespeople and service businesses
- Small business owners in the trades industry

**Why It Exists:**
Contractors spend their time on job sites, not marketing. SnapPost Pro eliminates friction by automating social media posting, allowing contractors to maintain an active Facebook presence without leaving the job site.
