## Full Description (for Meta App Review)

### What is SnapPost Pro?
SnapPost Pro is a mobile-first web application designed specifically for contractors, construction professionals, and tradespeople to streamline their social media marketing on Facebook.

### Core Functionality
SnapPost Pro enables contractors to:

1. **Upload Job Site Photos** — Capture photos directly from job sites using their mobile device
2. **Generate AI-Powered Captions** — Automatically create professional, branded captions tailored to their business
3. **Add Brand Overlays** — Apply company logos and branding to photos for consistent visual identity
4. **Post Directly to Facebook** — Publish branded content to their Facebook business page with a single click

### Who Uses It?
- Contractors (roofing, deck building, landscaping, plumbing, electrical, etc.)
- Construction companies
- Tradespeople and service professionals
- Small business owners in the trades industry

### Why It Matters
Contractors spend time on job sites, not marketing. SnapPost Pro eliminates the friction of manual social media posting by automating caption generation and branding, allowing contractors to maintain an active Facebook presence without leaving the job site.

### Permissions Requested
- **Facebook Login** — To authenticate users and verify their identity
- **Manage Pages** — To post content to the contractor's Facebook business page
- **Manage Posts** — To create and publish branded job site photos

### Data Privacy
SnapPost Pro does not store, sell, or share user data. All photos and captions are processed securely and only used to create Facebook posts as requested by the user. Users can delete their account and all associated data at any time.

### Compliance
- Privacy Policy: https://snappostpro.com/privacy
- Data Deletion: https://snappostpro.com/delete-account
